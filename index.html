4-Step After-Sales Workflow

Every service request follows a defined sequence ensuring nothing slips through the cracks, from the first call to the final invoice.

01
📞
Step 1

Call Booking

Log and capture every inbound service request with full customer and product context.

  • Customer name, contact & location
  • Product model & serial number
  • Purchase date & dealer details
  • Complaint description
  • Preferred service date & slot
  • Auto-generate Service Request ID
02
🛡️
Step 2

Confirm FOC or Chargeable

Instantly verify warranty status against the database and classify the request before any work begins.

  • Warranty period lookup (auto)
  • Check AMC / service contract
  • Identify exclusions (physical damage)
  • Tag as FOC or Paid
  • Notify customer of status
  • Manager approval for exceptions
03
🔧
Step 3

Spare / Service / Both

Determine what resources are required — spares, labour, or a combination — before dispatching or ordering.

  • Diagnose: only spare parts needed
  • Diagnose: only labour / service
  • Diagnose: both spares + service
  • Check stock availability in ERP
  • Raise Purchase Request if out of stock
  • Assign technician & schedule visit
04
📄
Step 4

Service Quotation

Generate a transparent, itemised quotation with spare costs and labour charges before closing the order.

  • Itemise spare parts with MRP / cost
  • Add labour charges per rate card
  • Apply FOC waiver if applicable
  • Include taxes (GST / VAT)
  • Send to customer for approval
  • Convert to Service Order on accept
Warranty Decision
FOC vs. Chargeable — Decision Matrix

The ERP checks warranty status automatically at Step 2. Here's what determines each outcome.

✓ FOC — Free of Charge

Covered Under Warranty / AMC

  • 📅 Within standard warranty period
  • 📋 Active Annual Maintenance Contract
  • 🏷️ Manufacturing defect confirmed
  • 🤝 Customer holds valid extended warranty
  • 📍 Fault covered under product recall
$ Chargeable

Outside Warranty / Not Covered

  • ⌛ Warranty period expired
  • 💥 Physical / accidental damage
  • 🔌 Damage due to misuse or power surge
  • 🛠️ Unauthorised prior repair
  • 📦 Consumable parts (not under warranty)
Resource Classification
What Does the Customer Need?

After diagnosis, the service engineer selects one of three types. This drives inventory, scheduling, and the final quotation.

📦

Spare Parts Only

Component replacement needed with no additional labour. Parts are issued from warehouse stock.

👨‍🔧

Service Only

Labour, calibration, cleaning, or software update with no spare part replacement required.

🔩

Spare + Service

Both parts replacement and technician labour are needed. Combined quotation is generated.
